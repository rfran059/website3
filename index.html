About

I'm a mechanical engineering student at the University of California, Riverside, with a passion for aerodynamics, simulation, and experimental validation. As a member of the Highlander Racing Formula SAE team, I design and analyze aerodynamic components — from multi-element front wing packages to full vehicle body profiles — using CFD and wind tunnel testing to drive performance improvements.

My experience spans computational analysis, rapid prototyping, and hands-on manufacturing. I've built everything from value-driven HiFi speakers and smart healthcare prototypes to CNC-machined components — always focused on merging rigorous engineering analysis with creative, manufacturable design.

Projects

Formula SAE Front Wing Package

Led aerodynamic design of a multi-element front wing for the Highlander Racing FSAE car. Validated iterations using CFD and optimized chassis-to-wing mounting structures with Ansys FEA for weight and strength. Designed an adaptable airfoil testing rig for experimental validation.

ANSYS CFD SolidWorks

Value-Driven HiFi Speakers

Produced two-way loudspeakers competing with premium brands at a fraction of cost. Developed a passive crossover using VituixCAD and designed a 19-part interlocking 3D-printed bass reflex enclosure. Achieved ±3dB frequency response and less than 0.1% THD across the audible spectrum.

3D Printing VituixCAD Acoustics

Smart Pill Bottle Prototype

Designed a low-cost pill bottle prototype for BearHack 2026 that tracks medication usage via real-time mass measurement. Built a load cell substitute using a potentiometer and 3D-printed spring mechanism. Implemented Arduino-based embedded logic with a user-facing display for dosage tracking and alerts.

Arduino 3D Printing Embedded Systems

FEA of Chassis Component

Performed static structural analysis on FSAE chassis tubular space frame components under vehicle-derived load cases. Evaluated factor of safety for multiple tube sizing options and identified optimal design criteria for weight reduction.

ANSYS FEA SolidWorks

Suspension Kinematics Study

Modeled a double-wishbone suspension in MATLAB to analyze kinematic properties including roll center, instant center, and anti-squat. Evaluated suspension geometry changes for improved handling characteristics.

MATLAB Python Vehicle Dynamics

Thermal Management System

Analyzed a thermal management system for FSAE power electronics. Evaluated heat rejection requirements and designed cooling loop layout for optimal thermal performance under track duty cycles.

ANSYS MATLAB Thermodynamics
